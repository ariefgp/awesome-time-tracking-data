![actiTime Logo](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/673473202f01ae5c847dfbe1/673473202f01ae5c847e0075_Timing%20logo.png)

## Overview
actiTime is a comprehensive time tracking tool designed to streamline workflows and enhance productivity. It is available across web, desktop, and mobile platforms, making it accessible for users in various settings.

## Features
- **Time Tracking**: Efficiently log time using intuitive timesheets.
- **Project Tracking**: Monitor progress and manage tasks effortlessly.
- **Budget Tracking**: Keep track of project costs and budgets.
- **Reports & Data Analysis**: Generate detailed reports for insightful data analysis.
- **Billing & Invoicing**: Simplify billing processes with integrated invoicing.
- **Team Management**: Manage team performance and assignments effectively.
- **Leave Management**: Oversee leave requests and approvals.
- **Custom Workflow**: Tailor workflows to suit unique organizational needs.

## Integrations
- **actiPLANS**: Seamless integration with actiPLANS for enhanced planning.
- **Chrome Plugin**: Access time management features directly from your browser.
- **QuickBooks**: Connect with QuickBooks for synchronized financial data.
- **Zapier**: Automate workflows by connecting with various applications via Zapier.
