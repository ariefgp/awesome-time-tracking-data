# All Hours

![All Hours Logo](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/673473202f01ae5c847dfbe1/673473202f01ae5c847e00a4_allhours-logo-web%20(1).svg)

All Hours is a comprehensive Time & Attendance management solution tailored for medium to large businesses. It integrates seamlessly with multiple platforms, offering a robust suite of features to streamline workforce management.

## Features

- **Flexible Clocking Options**: Utilize terminals, mobile, and web apps for clocking in and out, ensuring time tracking is accessible from anywhere.
- **Real-Time Tracking**: Capture work hours, absences, and presence data instantly.
- **Smart Scheduling**: Plan shifts efficiently, manage availability, and minimize scheduling conflicts.
- **Leave Management**: Simplify leave requests, approvals, and balance tracking for employees and managers.
- **Overtime Tracking**: Monitor and approve overtime transparently, ensuring clear policies and accurate records.
- **Automated Timesheets**: Convert clock-ins into reliable records, ready for payroll processing without manual intervention.
- **Payroll Integration**: Export accurate data quickly for payroll processing and connect with existing HR tools via API.
- **Absence Planning**: Plan and track absences accurately for efficient workforce management.
- **Custom Accruals**: Set up accruals compliant with labor laws and internal policies.
- **Audit Logs**: Maintain a detailed log of all changes for compliance and transparency.
- **Multi-Language Support**: Available in multiple languages, with ongoing additions.
