# Attendance Tracker

## Overview
The **Attendance Tracker** is an application designed to efficiently manage and record employee attendance, ensuring accurate records of work hours and absences. It serves as a tool to aid businesses in maintaining compliance and streamlining human resources processes.

## Features
- **Web-Based Tracking:** Log attendance data from any location with internet access.
- **Comprehensive Reporting:** Generate detailed reports by student or class, exportable to Excel or PDF.
- **Versatility:** Suitable for a wide range of uses, including educational settings, church groups, martial arts classes, and more.
- **User-Friendly Setup:** Easily set up students and classes to begin tracking immediately.

## Pricing
- **Free:** The basic attendance tracking and reporting functionalities are available at no cost.
