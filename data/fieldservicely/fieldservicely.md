# FieldServicely

**Category:** Field Team Management  
**Tags:** GPS, Real-Time, Geofencing

---

**Description:**  
FieldServicely is a cutting-edge GPS-based time tracking application specifically designed for field employees. This tool allows businesses to effectively monitor employee locations and tasks in real-time, facilitating efficient management of time on job sites.

---

## Features

- **Real-Time Tracking:** Monitor employee locations and tasks as they happen, ensuring transparency and efficient task management.
- **Geofenced Clock-In/Out:** Automate time tracking with geofencing technology, allowing employees to clock in and out only when they are within designated job site boundaries.
- **Task Management:** Assign and track tasks to ensure that field operations run smoothly and efficiently.
- **Location Monitoring:** Keep an eye on employee movements and ensure they remain within specified work zones.
