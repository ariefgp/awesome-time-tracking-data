## Overview
ProofHub is a comprehensive project management tool designed to integrate seamlessly into teams of all sizes. It supports efficient time tracking, project planning, and team collaboration, making it an ideal solution for businesses looking to enhance productivity and streamline operations.

[Visit ProofHub](https://www.proofhub.com/)

## Features
- **Time Tracking:** Track time spent on tasks manually or with a timer. Set time estimates and analyze time spent for enhanced productivity.
- **Project Planning:** Visualize projects and tasks using Gantt charts, create dependencies, and adjust schedules as priorities shift.
- **Custom Workflows:** Tailor workflows to fit your teamâ€™s specific needs, ensuring flexibility and adaptability.
- **Task Management:** Organize tasks in a board view, track progress, and identify bottlenecks early.
- **Calendar View:** View tasks, events, and milestones in a calendar format to manage schedules efficiently.
- **Personalized Dashboard:** Utilize a personalized "Me View" with widgets like My Tasks, Agenda, and Notes for focused productivity.
- **Client Feedback Management:** Collect and manage client feedback using forms, converting requests into actionable tasks.
- **Custom Reports:** Generate custom reports to gain insights into project progress and resource utilization.
- **Collaboration Tools:** Enhance team communication and collaboration with integrated tools.
