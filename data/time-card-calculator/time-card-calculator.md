## Overview

The **Time Card Calculator** is an essential tool for both employees and employers to efficiently calculate work hours and wages based on time card entries. It simplifies the process of tracking time worked, ensuring accurate payroll management.

## Features

- **Time Clock Format**: Customize your time clock with AM/PM or 24-hour format.
- **Pay Period Configuration**: Set your pay period preferences to weekly, bi-weekly, etc.
- **Work Week Settings**: Define the number of workdays per week and time periods per day.
- **Breaks Management**: Auto credit or deduct breaks/lunch periods, with a maximum of 180 minutes per day.
- **Overtime Calculation**: Configure overtime settings, including standard OT and custom OT rates.
- **Daily OT Display**: View daily overtime hours.
- **Rounding Options**: Round decimal hours according to your preferences.
- **Data Entry Options**: Choose from number, text, voice, or tab entry modes.
- **Multiple Employee Support**: Manage time cards for multiple employees.
- **California Overtime Compliance**: Special settings for California overtime laws.
